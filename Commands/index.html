Economy

Economy Commands:
• Cash : Check your current cash balance. (Ex : Mcash | /cash)
• Bank : Check your current bank balance. (Ex : Mbank | /bank)
• Deposit : Deposit cash into your bank account. (Ex : Mdeposit <Amount>)
• Withdraw : Withdraw cash from your bank account. (Ex : Mwithdraw <Amount>)
• give : Give cash to another user. (Ex : Mgive <Mention> <Amount>)

Earning

List :
• Claim : Claim rewards. "Cooldown : 1h" (Ex : Mclaim)
• Work : Perform work to earn rewards. "Cooldown : 3h" (Ex : Mwork)
• Daily : Claim daily rewards. "Cooldown : 1day" (Ex : Mdaily)
• Weekly : Claim weekly rewards. "Cooldown : 1week" (Ex : Mweekly)
